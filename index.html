Our Vision

At Ag-channel, we strive to solve the biggest irrigation problem of farmers in Nigeria. We use renewable energy to provide our customers with reliable acess to water (irrigation), and we can do that with a single system. Our product has a high efficiency drip irrigation, so farmers can grow more without the panic to get a source of an irrigation system. Ag-Channel is an innovative startup delivering change in crop field irrigation system by leveraging on renewable energy source from the sun and automating irrigation through machine learning and artificial intelligence, Our product, known as IRRIBOT, is designed to carry out field irrigation as scheduled by the farmer. it is powered using renewable energy from the sun and can be operated remotely by the farmer via our mobile software. At Ag-channel, you can increase your crop yield by 30%, and reduce your water usage by 80% with a simple push of a button.
